My life is really balanced with traveling, hiking, and family. I find each of these to be fun and it is what keeps me happy and smiling in my everyday life.

Traveling is something I wanted to do since a child. Watching tv with my family and seeing lifestyles of other families has caught my interest from early on. I wanted to visit and see how people live their life around the world. So, now as an adult, I am fortunate enough to visit a handful of countries. It has strengthened the love I have for people and diversity. It was so much more that what I used to see in the television as a kid.

As I traveled one thing I tried to do as often as I can is to hike. It gives me great joy to see nature and exercise while at it. Although I have hiked majority of the mountains near where I live, I was still able to hike in cool places like Greece and Ethiopia.
